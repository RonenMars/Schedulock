## Description

Transform your lock screen into a daily planner. Schedulock generates beautiful wallpapers that show your calendar agenda at a glance.

Pick your favorite design template, choose your calendars, and wake up to a fresh wallpaper every morning. Six stunning templates range from minimal to neon glow, all rendered at pixel-perfect resolution for your exact device.

Features:
- 6 design templates: Minimal, Frosted Glass, Gradient Band, Editorial, Neon Glow, Split View
- Full calendar integration: choose which calendars to display
- Automatic daily generation via iOS Shortcuts
- Lock screen widgets for real-time agenda
- Background photo support with blur and overlay effects
- Typography, color, and layout customization
- Works completely offline — no data leaves your device
